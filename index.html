About Surprise Furniture

Born from a love of wood and form, Surprise Furniture started as a small local workshop with a bold idea — transform spaces with thoughtful pieces. Today, we marry Arabic-inspired detail with modern function, delivering bed frames, cabinets, and bespoke wall installations that last generations.
